# 📘 FarmConnect – User Manual

## 🌾 Overview
FarmConnect is a digital platform that connects farmers directly with buyers, removing middlemen and ensuring fair pricing.

---

# 👨‍🌾 Farmer Guide

## Step 1: Register
- Open the FarmConnect app
- Click on **"Farmer Signup"**
- Enter:
  - Name
  - Village / Location
  - Contact details

---

## Step 2: Login
- Enter credentials
- Access Farmer Dashboard

---

## Step 3: Add Products
- Click **"Add Product"**
- Enter:
  - Crop name (e.g., Tomato, Rice)
  - Quantity
  - Price per kg
- Submit listing

---

## Step 4: Manage Orders
- View buyer requests
- Accept or reject orders
- Update stock automatically

---

# 🛒 Buyer Guide

## Step 1: Register
- Create buyer account

---

## Step 2: Browse Products
- View available crops
- Filter by:
  - Price
  - Location
  - Quantity

---

## Step 3: Place Order
- Select product
- Add quantity
- Click **"Order Now"**

---

## Step 4: Track Orders
- View order status:
  - Pending
  - Approved
  - Delivered

---

# 🧑‍💼 Admin Guide (Optional Feature)

## Responsibilities
- Monitor users
- Approve or remove listings
- Handle disputes

---

# 📊 Dashboard Features

## Farmer Dashboard
- Add/Edit products
- View orders
- Track earnings

## Buyer Dashboard
- View products
- Place orders
- Track purchases

---

# ⚠️ Important Notes
- Prices are controlled by farmers
- No middlemen allowed
- Real-time updates may be simulated in MVP
