# 1. Introduction

FarmConnect is a digital marketplace designed to connect farmers directly with consumers. The platform eliminates intermediaries, allowing farmers to receive fair prices while consumers gain access to fresh agricultural products at reasonable costs.

## Objectives

- Enable direct farmer-to-consumer sales
- Improve transparency in agricultural trade
- Reduce dependency on middlemen
- Provide an easy-to-use digital marketplace
- Support local farming communities

# 3. User Roles

FarmConnect supports three user roles:

## Farmer

Farmers can:

- Create and manage product listings
- Update inventory
- View customer orders
- Track sales performance
- Respond to customer reviews

## Consumer

Consumers can:

- Browse products
- Search and filter products
- Place orders
- Track order status
- Submit ratings and reviews

## Administrator

Administrators can:

- Manage users
- Monitor products
- Handle reports
- View analytics
- Manage platform settings

# 4. Registration

## Creating an Account

### Step 1

Open the FarmConnect website.

### Step 2

Click the **Register** button.

### Step 3

Enter:

- Full Name
- Email Address
- Mobile Number
- Password
- User Role (Farmer or Consumer)

### Step 4

Click **Create Account**.

### Step 5

Login using your registered credentials.

---

# 5. Login

## Accessing Your Account

1. Open the Login page.
2. Enter your email address.
3. Enter your password.
4. Click **Login**.

If login is successful, you will be redirected to your dashboard.

---

# 6. Farmer Guide

## Farmer Dashboard

The dashboard provides:

- Product management
- Order management
- Sales overview
- Notifications

---

## Adding a Product

### Step 1

Navigate to **Products**.

### Step 2

Click **Add Product**.

### Step 3

Enter:

- Product Name
- Category
- Description
- Price
- Quantity Available
- Product Image
- Location

### Step 4

Click **Save Product**.

The product will now appear in the marketplace.

---

## Managing Products

Farmers can:

- Edit product information
- Update stock quantity
- Delete products
- View product performance

---

## Managing Orders

Farmers can:

- View incoming orders
- Accept orders
- Reject orders
- Update order status
- Mark orders as delivered

# 7. Consumer Guide

## Browsing Products

Consumers can:

- View available products
- Search by keyword
- Filter by category
- Sort by price

---

## Product Details

Each product page displays:

- Product Name
- Description

## Placing an Order

### Step 1

Select a product.

### Step 2

Choose the desired quantity.

### Step 3

Click **Add to Cart**.

### Step 4

Proceed to Checkout.

### Step 5

Confirm your order.

---

## Payment Process

Supported payment methods:

- UPI
- Credit Card
- Debit Card
- Net Banking
- Cash on Delivery (Optional)

After successful payment, an order confirmation message will be displayed.

# 8. Order Management

## Order Status Types

### Pending

Order has been placed but not yet accepted.

### Accepted

Farmer has accepted the order.

### Processing

Order is being prepared.

### Shipped

Order is on the way.

### Delivered

Order has been successfully delivered.

### Cancelled

Order has been cancelled.

---

## Tracking Orders

Consumers can track orders from:

Dashboard → My Orders

# 11. Frequently Asked Questions

## Q1. How do I become a farmer seller?

Register as a Farmer during account creation.

---

## Q2. Can I edit my products later?

Yes. Farmers can edit product information at any time.

---

## Q3. How can I cancel an order?

Go to My Orders and select Cancel Order if the order has not been shipped.

---

## Q4. What payment methods are supported?

UPI, Debit Cards, Credit Cards, and Net Banking.

---

## Q5. Can consumers leave reviews?

Yes. Reviews can be submitted after order completion.

# 12. Troubleshooting

## Unable to Login

Check:

- Internet connection
- Email address
- Password correctness

If the issue persists, reset your password.

---

## Product Not Appearing

Verify:

- Product details are complete
- Product stock is greater than zero
- Product has been successfully saved

---

## Payment Failed

Try:

- Checking internet connectivity
- Verifying payment details
- Retrying after a few minutes
